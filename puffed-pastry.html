Puffed Pastry with Ham and Cheese

Description

This simple treat is a great appetizer or a delicious addition to Sunday tea.

Ingredients

  • Puffed pastry sheets
  • 8 slices of honey ham
  • 8 slices of swiss cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 F.
  2. Thaw the pastry sheets and lay flat on a cutting board.
  3. Roll out the first cutting sheet.
  4. Place down a slice of ham and then cheese, and continue to lay down these ingredients.
  5. Layer a second pastry sheet on top of the first layer.
  6. Pinch the layers shut using a fork. Using a knife, cut two slits at the top of the first layer.
  7. Coat the top of the puffed pastry using a beaten egg.
  8. Bake the puffed pastry for 18-20 minutes.
