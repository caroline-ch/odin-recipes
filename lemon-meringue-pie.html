Lemon Meringue Pie

Description

This lemon meringue pie a perfect treat for the summer. The crunchy graham cracker pie crust is filled with a layer of rich and creamy lemon filling.

This is one of the simplest pies to bake and can be made using store bought or homemade pie crust.

Ingredients

Pie Crust

  • 10 graham crackers
  • 3 TBSPs of butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

Pie Filling

  • 2 cups lemon juice
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 carton of whipping cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 F.
  2. In a large bowl, finely crush the graham crackers either using a food processor or hand crush the crackers in a zip lock bag.
  3. After crushing the crackers, incorporate softened butter and the brown sugar into the bowl. Mix until a dough-like mixture is formed.
  4. Form the mixture in a pie tin, ensuring that the walls of the crust have a 2 cm thickness on each side.
  5. In another large bowl, combine the lemon juice and the condensed milk and stir until well incorporated.
  6. Pour the filling into the pie crust and let bake in the oven for 40 minutes or until the filling is firm.
  7. While the pie bakes, whisk the whipping cream in a bowl until stiff peaks are formed and top the finished pie with the whipped cream.
