Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes are super easy to make, and are especially a great side dish for the holiday season.

Enjoy making this creamy filling dish for yourself or for your friends and family!

Ingredients

  • 8 large russet potatoes
  • 6 TBSPs of sour cream
  • 2 TSPs of chopped chives
  • 2 TSPs of cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Dice each potato into 2 cm cubes and add to a large pot filled with water. The water should cover the potatoes by 2 inches.
  2. Bring the pot to a boil and leave fire on high for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Once the potatoes are tender, drain the pot with about 1/4th cup of water left in the pot.
  4. Using a fork, mash the potatoes. Stir in the sour cream until well-incorporated in the mixture.
  5. Top the mashed potatoes with shredded cheddar cheese and the chives.
